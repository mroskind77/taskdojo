Common Questions

Q: How long does it take to reach Black Belt?  
A: 1-2 years for most users with consistent effort. Varies by age category and consistency.

Q: Can I lose my belt if I take a break?  
A: No! Belts are permanent. Your progress is never lost.

Q: What if my friend advances faster than me?  
A: They may have a higher age multiplier, better streak, or more time. Focus on your own journey.

Q: Can I skip belts?  
A: No. You must earn each belt in sequence. No shortcuts to mastery!

Q: Do I need to "test" for each belt?  
A: No! Belts are awarded automatically when you reach the required points.

Q: What happens after Black Belt?  
A: You maintain your Black Belt and continue building discipline. Future updates may add "degrees" of Black Belt (1st degree, 2nd degree, etc.).

Q: Does my belt affect anything besides visuals?  
A: Certain belts unlock avatar skins and achievements. Future features may include belt-specific challenges.
