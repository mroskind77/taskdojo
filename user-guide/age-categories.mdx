Age Categories

Overview

Task Dojo uses age categories to adjust belt progression and point requirements to match developmental stages. This ensures everyone gets an appropriately challenging and rewarding experience, regardless of age.

For Parents & Senseis

Choosing the Right Category for Your Disciple

Consider:
Chronological age (starting point)
Developmental stage (may differ from age)
Current executive function skills
Support needs and independence level

When to adjust:
If your child is significantly ahead or behind their age peers in executive function, choose the category that matches their current developmental stage, not just their age.

Examples:
Advanced 10-year-old: Consider Stickbug (13-15) if managing complex responsibilities
Neurodivergent teen: May benefit from Cricket (9-12) templates despite being 14
Young adult with EF challenges: Mantis (16+) but start with simpler feats

Common Questions

Q: Can I change my age category after onboarding?  
A: Not currently within the app. Contact support if you need to adjust.

Q: What if I'm between categories?  
A: Choose the one that feels closest to your current needs. You can always adjust preset templates.

Q: Does age category affect anything besides points?  
A: Yes! It also customizes preset templates, suggested feat complexity, and progression pace.

Q: Will my age category automatically update on my birthday?  
A: Not yet, but this feature is planned for future updates.

Q: I'm 25 but struggle with executive function. Should I choose a younger category?  
A: No. Choose Mantis and use simpler feats. The 2.0x multiplier will help you progress appropriately while building skills.
