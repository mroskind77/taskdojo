What is Task Dojo?

Task Dojo is a gamified task management app designed to help people (especially kids and those with executive functioning challenges) manage their time and tasks better through martial arts-themed gamification.

Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Three User Types" icon="users">
Masters: Independent users who manage their own tasks
Senseis: Mentors or parents who guide and approve tasks for disciples
Disciples: Guided learners who require approval for completed tasks
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Belt Progression System" icon="award">
    Earn points through task completion and progress through 8 belt levels from White to Black belt, with age-adjusted requirements and quality-based scoring.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Streak Tracking & Grace Tokens" icon="fire">
    Build momentum with daily streaks, earn bonus points, and use Grace Tokens or Streak Savior to maintain your progress during challenging days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Task Breakdown" icon="brain">
    Break down overwhelming tasks into manageable steps using AI assistance, making complex feats feel achievable.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Accessibility First" icon="universal-access">

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Focus Timer (Pomodoro)" icon="clock">

Why Task Dojo?

Task Dojo was built on research-backed principles for supporting executive functioning skills:

Empathetic Voice: Validates struggles while providing clear guidance
Age-Appropriate: Four age categories with tailored point multipliers
Quality Over Quantity: Encourages thoughtful completion with quality ratings
Supportive Gamification: Celebrates progress without punitive mechanics
Parent-Child Collaboration: Sensei-Disciple system for guided learning
