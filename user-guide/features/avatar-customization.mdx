Common Questions


Q: Can I use custom images as avatars?  
A: Not currently. Avatars are emoji-based for consistency and accessibility.



Q: Can I change my avatar unlimited times?  
A: Yes! Change as often as you want among unlocked avatars.



Q: Do avatars give any bonuses?  
A: No. Avatars are purely cosmetic and recognitional.



Q: Can I see other users' avatars?  
A: Only if you're their Sensei (for disciples). Otherwise profiles are private.



Q: What if I regret my starting avatar choice?  
A: Change it anytime in Settings! You're not locked in.



Q: Can I unlock avatars out of order?  
A: Yes. Belt avatars unlock in sequence, but achievement avatars can unlock in any order.



Q: Is Black Belt Master the rarest avatar?  
A: Yes, along with future ultra-rare avatars for extreme achievements.
