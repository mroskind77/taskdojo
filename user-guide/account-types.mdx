Common Questions

Q: Can one person have both Master and Disciple accounts?  
A: Not currently. Each account is one type. However, Senseis maintain their own Master progress while managing disciples.

Q: How many disciples can a Sensei manage?  
A: There's no hard limit, but we recommend focusing on quality feedback for 1-5 disciples.

Q: Do disciples need their own email?  
A: Senseis create disciple accounts, so disciples don't need their own email addresses.

Q: Can disciples see other disciples' progress?  
A: No. Each disciple only sees their own progress.

Q: What if I don't have someone to be my Sensei?  
A: Choose Master! The system works great for independent users too.
