Common Questions

Q: How often should I check Insights?

Q: What's the most important metric?  
A: No single metric. Look at the full picture‚Äîcompletion rate, quality, streaks together.

Q: Should I share Insights with my disciple?  
A: Selectively. Show positive trends to celebrate. Discuss struggles empathetically.

Q: Can disciples see their own Insights?  
A: Not currently. This is a Sensei-only tool. Future may add disciple-facing analytics.

Q: What if all metrics are bad?

A: Sort of. More data = better insights. Need at least 10-20 completions for meaningful patterns.
