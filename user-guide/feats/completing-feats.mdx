Points Calculation

The Formula

When you complete a feat, points are calculated using this formula:
```

Total Points = Base Points √ó Quality Multiplier √ó Timeliness Multiplier √ó (1 + Streak Bonus) √ó Age Multiplier

```

Let's break it down:

---

1. Base Points (Difficulty)

Set when you create the feat:
Easy: 10 points
Medium: 25 points
Hard: 50 points
Epic: 100 points

---

2. Quality Multiplier

Based on your rating:
Poor: 0.5x
Fair: 0.75x
Good: 1.0x
Excellent: 1.5x

---

3. Timeliness Multiplier

Current Status: Not yet implemented (coming soon!)

Planned:
Early: +20% (completed before due date)
On-time: +10% (completed on due date)
No penalty: (completed within 24 hours after due)
Late: -10% (completed 1-3 days late)
Very late: -25% (completed >3 days late)

---

4. Streak Bonus

Based on current streak:
0-2 days: No bonus
3-6 days: +10%
7-13 days: +20%
14-20 days: +30%
21-29 days: +40%
30+ days: +50% (maximum!)

Example: 7-day streak = multiply by 1.2 (20% bonus)

---

5. Age Multiplier

Based on your age category:
Grasshopper (5-8): 1.0x
Cricket (9-12): 1.2x
Stickbug (13-15): 1.5x
Mantis (16+): 2.0x

Common Completion Questions

Q: What if I forget to mark a feat complete?  
A: Mark it complete the next day! It still counts, though it might affect streaks if you go 24+ hours without completing anything.

Q: Can I change my quality rating after submitting?  
A: Not currently. Choose thoughtfully before submitting.

Q: What if I only partially completed a feat?  
A: Either rate it "Poor" and still complete, or don't mark it complete and try again tomorrow.

Q: Do I need to complete feats in order?  
A: No! Complete them in whatever order works for you.

Q: Can I complete the same feat multiple times?  
A: Not yet. Create duplicate feats if you need recurring tasks, or use templates.

Q: How long does Sensei approval take?  
A: Varies by Sensei, but most approve within 24 hours. Be patient!

Q: What happens if Sensei never approves?  
A: Feat stays "Pending." Talk to your Sensei! If needed, contact support.
