Troubleshooting

Settings Not Saving

Solution: Check browser local storage permissions. Task Dojo requires local storage to save your preferences.

Font Not Changing

Solution: 
Hard refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
Clear browser cache
Try a different browser

Animations Still Showing with Reduce Motion On

Solution:
Verify "Reduce Motion" toggle is ON in Settings
Check system-level "Reduce Motion" setting
Hard refresh the page

High Contrast Not Enough Contrast

Solution: Enable both High Contrast Mode AND increase your browser zoom (Ctrl/Cmd + Plus).
