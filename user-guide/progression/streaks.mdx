Grace Tokens System

What Are Grace Tokens?

Grace Tokens are your safety net for when life happens. They give you a chance to save your streak or start fresh without shame.

You earn:
2 Grace Tokens per rolling 7-day window
Tokens reset on a rolling basis (not weekly on a specific day)
Use them or lose them‚Äîthey don't stack infinitely

Example rolling window:
Day 1: Earn 2 tokens
Day 8: Tokens reset (2 new tokens)
Day 15: Tokens reset again

Common Streak Questions

Q: What time does the streak "day" reset?  
A: Midnight in your timezone. Complete a feat between 12:00 AM and 11:59 PM each day.

Q: Can I complete multiple feats but only count as one day?  
A: Yes. You need at least 1 feat per day. Extra feats earn more points but don't extend the streak count faster.

Q: Do Grace Tokens stack if I don't use them?  
A: No. You get 2 per rolling 7-day window, maximum. Use them or lose them.

Q: Can I "bank" completions to skip days?  
A: No. You must complete at least 1 feat each day to maintain the streak.

Q: What happens if I forget and my streak breaks?  
A: Streak Savior modal appears. Use a Grace Token for Rescue Quest, or Begin Fresh.

Q: Does Rescue Quest have to be a specific feat?  
A: No! Just 30 minutes of focused work on any feat or task.

Q: Can I pause my streak for vacation?  
A: Not currently. Either maintain it with quick feats, use Grace Tokens, or start fresh when back.

Q: How do I see my longest streak ever?  
A: Check your profile for "Longest Streak" stat.
