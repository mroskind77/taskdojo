Create predictable routines:

Batch approvals by time:
Set aside 10-15 minutes
Review all pending approvals at once

Reduces context switching

Disciples with broken streaks

Common Questions

Q: Can I delete a disciple account?  
A: Contact support. Deletion should be rare and thoughtful.

Q: Can disciples see each other?  
A: No. Each disciple only sees their own data.

Q: Can I transfer a disciple to another Sensei?  
A: Not currently. Contact support if needed.

Q: What if disciple forgets their password?  
A: You can reset it since you created the account. Use the email you provided.

Q: Can one child have multiple Sensei accounts?  
A: No. One disciple account = one Sensei. But you can share Sensei duties informally.

Q: How do I archive inactive disciples?  
A: Not currently supported. Inactive disciples remain in list.

Q: Can disciples become Senseis later?  
A: Not directly. They'd need a new Sensei account. But they can transition to Master (future feature).

Q: What's the ideal age to start a disciple?  
A: Grasshopper age (5-8) works if they can read basic words. Adjust to individual readiness.
