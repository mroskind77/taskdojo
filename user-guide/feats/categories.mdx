Common Questions

Q: Can a feat belong to multiple categories?  
A: No. Each feat belongs to one category. If it spans multiple times, choose primary or break into separate feats.

Q: What if I do homework in the morning?  
A: Put it in "After-school" category anyway (it's homework), or create a custom "Morning homework" feat in Morning category‚Äîyour choice!

Q: Do I have to use all four categories?  
A: No! Use what works for you. Some users only use Morning and Nighttime.

Q: Can I rename categories?  
A: Not currently. They're fixed to maintain consistency across users.

Q: Do categories affect points?  
A: No. Categories are organizational only. Points come from difficulty, quality, streaks, and age.

Q: What category for weekend tasks?  
A: Use whatever makes sense! Morning chores = Morning. Weekend project = After-school. Bedtime = Nighttime.
