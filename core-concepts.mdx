Terminology

Feats (Not Tasks)

Task Dojo uses "feats" instead of "tasks" or "missions" in all user-facing copy. This terminology is intentional and research-backed:

Empowering: Frames activities as achievements
Action-oriented: Emphasizes accomplishment
Non-clinical: Avoids language that might feel like treatment
Motivating: Celebrates what you're capable of

<Note>
In technical documentation and code, you'll still see "task" - but always use "feat" when addressing users.
</Note>

Progression System

Points

Points are the primary currency of progression in Task Dojo. You earn points by completing feats, and points determine your belt level.

Point Calculation Formula:

```
Base = Feat Points √ó (1 + Quality + Streak + Timeliness)
Final = Base √ó Age Multiplier
```

Example:

A Stickbug (13-17) completes a 10-point feat with:
Quality: 5 stars (+20%)
Streak: 15 days (+20%)
Timeliness: 15 minutes early (+10%)

```
Base = 10 √ó (1 + 0.20 + 0.20 + 0.10) = 10 √ó 1.50 = 15 points
Final = 15 √ó 1.2 (age multiplier) = 18 points earned
```

Streaks

A streak is the number of consecutive days you've completed at least one feat.

Streak Benefits:
Bonus points on feat completion
Achievement unlocks at milestones
Avatar skin unlocks
Builds momentum and habit

Streak Bonuses:
7+ days: +10% points
14+ days: +20% points
30+ days: +30% points

Streak Rules:
Complete any feat to maintain streak
Streak breaks if you miss a day
Grace Tokens can prevent breaks
Streak Savior offers last-chance protection

Grace Tokens

Grace Tokens provide streak protection:

Receive: 2 tokens every 7 days (rolling window)
Use: Automatically applied if you miss a day
Effect: Preserves your streak
Limit: Can't stockpile (max 2 at a time)

<Warning>
Grace Tokens are used automatically. You can't choose when to use them - they activate if you're about to lose your streak.
</Warning>

Streak Savior

When your streak is about to break and you're out of Grace Tokens, Streak Savior appears:

Offer: One-time option to save your streak
Cost: -50% points on next feat completion
When: Only offered once per streak break
Choice: Accept penalty or start fresh

Approval Workflow (Disciples Only)

For Disciples, completing a feat is a two-step process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Disciple Completes Feat">
Disciple marks feat as complete
Selects quality rating (optional)
Feat enters "Pending Approval" state
No points awarded yet
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sensei Reviews">
Sensei receives notification
Views feat completion
Can adjust quality rating
Can add a guidance note
Approves or rejects
  </Step>

  <Step title="Points Awarded">
    If approved:
Points calculated with final quality rating
Points added to Disciple's total
Belt level updated if threshold crossed
Streak incremented
    
    If rejected:
No points awarded
Feat returns to incomplete state
Disciple can try again
  </Step>
</Steps>
