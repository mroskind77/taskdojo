Common Sensei Questions

Q: How many disciples can I manage?  
A: No hard limit, but 1-5 recommended for quality feedback.

Q: Can I be both Sensei and Disciple?  
A: Not currently. You can be a Sensei (which includes Master capabilities) or a Disciple, but not both roles simultaneously.

Q: Do I have to approve every feat?  
A: Yes, disciples don't earn points until approved. This is the core of the accountability system.

Q: What if I'm busy and can't approve promptly?  
A: Set expectations with disciples. Batch approve once daily. But consistency matters‚Äîdon't leave them hanging for days.

Q: Can disciples see my feats?  
A: Not currently. Your feats are private unless you share your screen/progress with them.

Q: What if a disciple cheats or games the system?  
A: Have an honest conversation. Explain that they're only cheating themselves. Consider whether Task Dojo is the right tool for them right now.

Q: Can I transfer a disciple to another Sensei?  
A: Not currently. Contact support if you need to transfer.

Q: Do I earn points for being a Sensei?  
A: Yes‚Äîyour own points from your own feats! Plus there are Sensei-specific achievements.
