Best Practices for AI Breakdown

Review Every Suggestion

Why: AI is a starting point, not the final answer

How:
Read each sub-feat
Ask: "Does this make sense for me?"
Adjust to your specific needs
Remove steps you don't need

---

Maintain Logical Order

Why: Some steps must happen before others

Check:
Research before writing
Gather materials before creating
Draft before editing
Setup before execution

Reorder if needed - Click and drag sub-feats to rearrange

---

Right-Size Sub-Feats

Too big:
Sub-feat still feels overwhelming
>30 minutes estimated
Solution: Break that sub-feat down further!

Too small:
Sub-feat is trivial (<2 minutes)
Creates unnecessary overhead
Solution: Combine with another step

Just right:
5-30 minutes estimated
Clear, actionable
Feels achievable

---

Complete in Order (Usually)

Why sequential order matters:
Builds on previous work
Logical progression
Prevents rework

Example:
Don't jump to "Edit essay" before "Write essay"!

Exception:
Some sub-feats are independent and can be done in any order (e.g., "Gather materials" and "Research background" might both be first-day tasks)

---

How:

Common Questions

Q: Does AI Breakdown cost extra?  
A: No! It's included in Task Dojo. (Note: Requires OpenAI API key configured by admin)

Q: Can I break down any feat, not just Hard/Epic?  
A: Yes! Click any feat and select "Break Down This Feat"

Q: What if AI suggestions don't make sense?  
A: Customize them! AI is a starting point. Adjust to your needs.

Q: Can I manually create sub-feats without AI?  
A: Yes! Click "Add Sub-Feat Manually" in the breakdown modal.

Q: Do sub-feats earn points separately?  
A: Yes! Each sub-feat awards points when completed.

Q: What happens if I don't finish all sub-feats?  
A: Parent feat stays incomplete. Complete sub-feats whenever you can!

Q: Can I delete a sub-feat?  
A: Yes! Delete any sub-feat just like a regular feat.

Q: Can I break down a sub-feat further?  
A: Yes! If a sub-feat still feels too big, break it down again.
