Common Questions

Q: Do achievements give points?  
A: No. Achievements are recognition, not point sources. They may unlock avatars though!

Q: Can I lose achievements?  
A: No. Once earned, achievements are permanent.

Q: Can I see friends' achievements?  
A: Only if you're their Sensei (for disciples) or they share their profile.

Q: What's the rarest achievement?  
A: Year of Discipline (365-day streak) and Black Belt Master are the rarest.

Q: Do achievements affect belt progression?  
A: No. Belts are based purely on points. Achievements are separate recognition.

Q: Can I hide achievements I don't care about?  
A: Not currently. All achievements visible in your achievement gallery.

Q: Are more achievements coming?  
A: Yes! Future updates will add seasonal achievements, challenge achievements, and more.
